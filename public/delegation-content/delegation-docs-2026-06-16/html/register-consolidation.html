A consolidation is used when you want to establish an ownership connection between two addresses, such as combining the Total Days Held (TDH) of multiple wallets that you control.

To create a consolidation between two wallets (e.g., Wallet A and Wallet B), both wallets must register a consolidation with each other.

This means that you need to register a delegation address with consolidation from Wallet A to Wallet B, and vice versa, from Wallet B to Wallet A.

To register a consolidation:

  1. Go to the Delegation Center section and click the Register Consolidation button.
  2. Complete the Register Consolidation Form:

    1. Choose the collection for which you want to register a consolidation (e.g., ‘Any Collection’ for all collections or a specific collection such as 'The Memes Collection').

    2. Enter the delegation address (e.g., a hot wallet address).

    3. Click the 'Submit' button and execute the transaction.

    Notes:

    1. Please bear in mind that consolidation is marked as incomplete when you initiate only one transaction ex. from Wallet A to Wallet B.

    2. In order for the consolidation to be marked as complete you need to register consolidation also from Wallet B to Wallet A.
