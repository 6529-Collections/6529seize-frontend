How to Revoke a Delegation/Delegation Manager/Consolidation?


  1. Go to the Delegation Center Section and click the 'View/Manage' button for (e.g., 'Any Collection' for all collections or a specific collection such as 'The Memes Collection').

  2. On the Manage/View Collection page, find the address you want to revoke and click the 'Revoke' button next to it.

  3. Execute the transaction.
