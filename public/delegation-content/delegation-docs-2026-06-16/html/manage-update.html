How to Update a Delegation/Delegation Manager/Consolidation?


  1. Go to the Delegation Center Section and click the 'View/Manage' button for (e.g., 'Any Collection' for all collections or a specific collection such as 'The Memes Collection').

  2. On the Manage/View Collection page, find the address you want to update and click the 'Update' button next to it.

  3. Fill out the Update Delegation form:

    • Enter the new address.

    • Select an expiry date.

    • Specify if the updated delegation applies to all tokens owned by the delegator or just a specific token.

  4. Click the 'Submit' button and execute the transaction.
