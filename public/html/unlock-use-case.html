How to unlock wallet on a specific usecase on a collection?


Select a collection from the main page of the Delegation Center. Navigate at the bottom of the Manage/View Collection Page and click on the Lock/Unlock Use Case dropdown list. Select a Usecase that is already locked and execute the transaction. Your wallet will be unlocked and it will start accepting incoming delegations on that specific usecase on the specific collection.
