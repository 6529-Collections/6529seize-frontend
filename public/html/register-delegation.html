How to Register a Delegation?


Click on the Register Delegation button that is located on the main page of the Delegations Center and then on the form that will appear select/insert the following:

  • Select the Collection for which you want to register the delegation
  • Insert the delegation address (ex. hot wallet)
  • Choose the specific usecase for which you will register the delegation
  • Select an Expiry Date
  • Specify if the delegation refers to all tokens owned by the Delegator or a specific token
  • Click the submit button and execute the transaction.
