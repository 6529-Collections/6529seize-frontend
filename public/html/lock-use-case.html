How to lock wallet on a specific usecase on a collection?


Select a collection from the main page of the Delegations Center. Navigate at the bottom of the Manage/View Collection Page and click on the Lock/Unlock Use Case dropdown list. Select a Usecase and execute the transaction. Your wallet will be locked and it will stop accepting incoming delegations on that specific usecase on the specific collection.

Note: If you want your wallet to not accept incoming delegations on a specific usecase for any collection then follow the same steps as described above using Any Collections.
